Polly.JS (introductory) no configuration and uni-directional dependencies

React (advanced) larger code base with some bi-directional dependencies

The case studies above investigate and demonstrate the capabilities of Eunice, by using it on well known open source projects.

The case studies aren't intended as commentaries or critiques of the projects. Choices made in the case studies are constrained by the authors understanding of the projects. Potentially more suitable choices might be made if Eunice was used by contributors to the projects. Suggestions to improve the case studies are welcome. Hopefully the case studies are helpful to contributors of the projects.
